Fixed Velocity Principle

Signals in PCB traces travel at a constant velocity (approx. 15cm/ns). Therefore, Time = Distance / Velocity. Longer traces inevitably result in later arrival times.

Why Timing Matters?

If the skew (arrival time difference) exceeds the setup/hold time of the receiver, the data is latched incorrectly, causing a system crash or data corruption.
