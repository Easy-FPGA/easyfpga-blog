Major FPGA Manufacturers

1.AMD (Acquired Xilinx in 2022)

  • Key Products: Spartan, Aritix, Kintex, Virtex, ZynQ, Versal

  • Xilinx was a long-time leader in the FPGA market and was acquired by AMD in 2022.

  • Provides high-performance FPGA and adaptive SoC (System-on-Chip) product lines. Produces products used in various fields such as data centers, communications, and automotive.

  1. **Intel (Acquired **Altera in 2015)

  • Key Products: Cyclone, Arria, Stratix, Agilex

  • Altera was acquired by Intel and integrated into the Intel FPGA business unit.

  • Provides high-performance FPGA and SoC product lines, with a strong presence in the data center and communications sectors.

  1. Lattice Semiconductor

  • Key Products: iCE, MachXO, ECP, CrossLink

  • Has strengths in the low-power, small-form-factor FPGA market.

  • Provides products specialized for various fields such as IoT (Internet of Things), mobile, and industrial automation.

  1. Microchip

  • Key Products: IGLOO, SmartFusion, PolarFire

  • Entered the FPGA market by acquiring Microsemi (formerly Actel).

  • Provides FPGA products specialized for high-reliability sectors such as defense, aerospace, and industrial control.

  1. Efinix

  • Key Products: Trion, Titanium

  • Efinix’s Quantum™ FPGA technology offers superior power efficiency, performance, and area advantages compared to traditional FPGA technologies.

  • Specialized in edge computing and AI acceleration.
